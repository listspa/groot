Toasts

Toast notification toasts can be used to display small success or error messages to the user. They are (relatively) non intrusive. Click on the following button to display some of them:

How to use them

To use the toasts you need to do two things:

  1. in your root component (generally AppComponent) add the appropriate tag: 
    <groot-notification-toast-list></groot-notification-toast-list>
  2. when you need to show one notification, inject the NotificationToastService and call its function addToast. You can also call the method showGenericSavedNotification (and the similar ones) to generate one of the commonly-used notification.

By default the toasts are added with the autoRemove set to true, meaning that they will be automatically hidden after 5 seconds.

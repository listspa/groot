Tabs look & feel

We use the standard component from Ngx-Bootstrap. We have customized the look & feel.

We also have implemented a directive grootTabOrder that you can use to force the order of the tabs. This is necessary to work around a bug in ngx-bootstrap when tabs have an *ngIf directive (or similar) and are added dynamically. Here's an example

A Title

Tab content Grooooooooooooooot Tab 3 Tab 4 

<tabset class="tabs-bordered">
  <tab heading="Hello" [grootTabOrder]="0">
    <h2>A Title</h2>
    Tab content
  </tab>
  <tab heading="World" [grootTabOrder]="2">
    Grooooooooooooooot
  </tab>
  <tab heading="Cruel" [grootTabOrder]="1">
    Tab 3
  </tab>
  <tab heading="I am not enabled" [disabled]="true" [grootTabOrder]="3">
    Tab 4
  </tab>
</tabset>

Cards

Bootstrap supports the cards to wrap some content, such as a form. It looks pretty good and can be useful if you have a page with a couple of forms. We do not have a specific component at the moment, but rather simply a css customization.

The header by default looks the same as a groot-collapsible-box. However you can customize its color by using the classes card-header-primary (and similar) following the card-header class.

Here's a sample card with a small form:

  <form #form="ngForm">
    <div class="card"
         style="width: 40rem">

      <div class="card-header">
        <h4>Card title</h4>
      </div>

      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="row">
          <div class="col-sm-8">
            <groot-input
              label="Name"
              name="name"
              [(ngModel)]="record.name"
              [required]="true"
            ></groot-input>
          </div>

          <div class="col-sm-4">
            <groot-checkbox
              label="Spam me!"
              name="spamMe"
              [(ngModel)]="record.spamMe"
            ></groot-checkbox>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card-footer">
        <div class="buttons-list-bottom-right">
          <groot-button
            label="common.cancel"
            icon="fa fa-times"
            (click)="resetForm()"
            classes="btn-outline-danger"
          ></groot-button>

          <groot-button
            label="common.save"
            icon="fa fa-check"
            (click)="submitForm()"
            [disabled]="form.invalid"
          ></groot-button>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </form>
